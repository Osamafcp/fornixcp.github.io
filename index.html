Our Mission

We drive scientific research into task-shifting community psychology practice. By delivering evidence-based psycho-educational programs, highly scalable community interventions, and advanced Research, Data & M&E services, we uplift individuals, communities, and organizations across Africa.

Rebuilding Africa for Africans through the African values of community stakeholder involvement, our low-cost Community Collaboration Intervention Model addresses deep-rooted challenges. By fostering resilient prosocial networks and focusing on key pillars of community development, we aim to generate lasting, tangible change that transforms every aspect of African welfare.

Signature Projects & Impact

Well-Being Studies
Implemented comprehensive pre/post surveys combined with correlational analysis to measure and deliver quantified improvements in individual well-being. Produced detailed reports and presentations that clearly demonstrated these gains, facilitating data-driven decision-making for stakeholders.

Psychosocial Support Roll-Out
Executed a scaled CCIM (Comprehensive Community Intervention Model) across Gauteng, achieving an unprecedented human resource expansion of 242.91%. Impact reports generated from this pilot program directly contributed to a statistically significant increase in repeat funding from Public Benefit Organizations (PBOs), validating the model’s effectiveness and sustainability.

Neuro Empowerment Pilot
Targeted students with neurodevelopmental disorders and those at high psychological risk through a tailored empowerment pilot program. This initiative fostered cyclical improvement in self-efficacy scores. The success of this pilot secured buy-in from clients and institutions, enabling a semester-long extension to expand and deepen impact.
